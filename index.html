Church of

the Holy

The Church of the Holy Sepulcher or the Church of the Holy Sepulcher is a church within the walls of the Old City of Jerusalem. The church was built over Golgotha or Golgotha, which is the site of the rock on which Jesus is believed to have been crucified. It is considered one of the holiest and most important Christian churches in the Christian world. According to Christian beliefs, the church contains the place where Jesus was buried, and its name is the Holy Sepulcher.
